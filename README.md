This project presents a **Business Process Analysis and Improvement Plan** for *Plympton Park Day & Night Surgery* in Adelaide, South Australia.
The clinic operates under a contractor-based business model, providing infrastructure, billing systems, and support services to General Practitioners (GPs).

The project focused on two critical business processes:

1. **Appointment Booking Process**
2. **Patient Attendance Process**

Through stakeholder interviews and BPMN 2.0 modelling, the team identified operational inefficiencies, system dependencies, and digital bottlenecks, proposing automation-based improvements aligned with Lean Healthcare and BPM principles.
